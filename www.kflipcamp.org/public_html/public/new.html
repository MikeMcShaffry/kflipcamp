Who in the hell is KFLIP?

RATED F - our station drops F-bombs all the time, so we might not be safe for younger or more gentle ears.

KFLIP started as a temporary, emergency news and entertainment radio station at Flipside, a regional burn even in Texas!

In May 2019, KFLIP broadcast the event on the Internet for the first time, and after that we (mostly) never went off the air.

We broadcast an eclectic mix of nearly all kinds of music - sometimes back to back. A few times a week we have live DJs, including 'Gryphons Aerie' on Monday nights and our 'Hot Mics and Dead Air' show on Friday nights.
